about

Lenke is a photographer based in Budapest, who was always drawn to the captivating look of dark and moody lighting, cinematic style that speaks volumes in her work.

Growing up, Lenke was mesmerized by how movies encapsulated the essence of fleeting moments, the interplay of lighting, colors, and composition, weaving stories that lingered long after the credits rolled. Inspired by this, Lenke embarked on her photographic journey, aiming to evoke similar vibes and colors through her lens.

If you're seeking a photographer who appreciates the artistry of color and lighting, and elevates photographs by infusing them with compelling narratives, you've come to the right place.

I invite you to experience a photoshoot like no other. Imagine being the star of your own cinematic story, with each shot capturing a scene that feels straight out of a film. My unique photography style ensures that your moments are transformed into beautifully edited frames, freezing time in a captivating and artistic way. Step into the spotlight and let's create something truly unforgettable together.
